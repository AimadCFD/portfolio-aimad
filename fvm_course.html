5. Conclusion and Applications

The Finite Volume Method is a versatile and effective approach for solving diffusion problems, particularly when dealing with complex geometries and boundary conditions. It can be applied to one-dimensional, two-dimensional, and three-dimensional problems, such as heat conduction in solids, species diffusion, and other transport phenomena.
